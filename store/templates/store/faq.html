Why bamboo?

Through our project, Irtiqa, we aim to provide an environmentally sustainable alternative to plastic and wooden products while promoting the growth of bamboo. Bamboo is one of the fastest growing plants on the planet. It regenerates naturally after harvesting, needs no irrigation and grows naturally pesticide free due to its antimicrobial properties. A grove of bamboo releases 35% more oxygen than an equivalent stand of trees.

Are bamboo products truly a good alternative to wooden products?

Bamboo is a viable replacement for wood. It can be harvested in 3-5 years versus 10-20 for most softwoods. It can out yield pine 6 to 1 in biomass production.

Who makes these products?

Our products are made by a community of bamboo artisans. All the money generated through the sale of our products is reinvested in the community to provide them with employment and to help them become self sufficient.
